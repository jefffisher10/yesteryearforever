CENTER

Home

Family is the center of my life.

My wife and my daughters matter more to me than any project I’ve ever built. Everything else — the systems, the writing, the experiments, the archive — exists inside that.

I come from a large, close family. We gather often, celebrate loudly, and stay connected across time. My dad’s passing changed that world, but not its importance.

If this site lasts, I want it to preserve not just what I built or thought about, but the life I was lucky enough to live among.
