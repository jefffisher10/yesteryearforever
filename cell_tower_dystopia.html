Cell Tower Dystopia

Cell towers have a technological dystopian feel especially when juxtaposed against a natural backdrop. This is a collection of cell tower images I took around my city. An ominous feeling hit me one day and I couldn't unsee it after that. It reminded me a lot of Simon Stålenhag's art, specifically from The Electric State.
